About Craft Logistics, Inc.

Craft Logistics specializes in delivering cost‑effective and reliable brokerage solutions, connecting businesses with trusted carriers and logistics partners worldwide. Our services are designed to meet the evolving needs of a dynamic customer base, ensuring smooth operations across every stage of the supply chain.

We provide a comprehensive suite of freight and logistics offerings, including LTL and FTL transportation, inter‑modal and 3PL solutions, customs clearance, warehousing, distribution, last‑mile delivery, and technology integration. With a focus on efficiency, transparency, and customer satisfaction, Craft Logistics is committed to helping businesses streamline their logistics strategy and achieve measurable savings in both cost and time.
