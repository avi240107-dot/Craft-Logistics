Our Services

Reliable freight brokerage and logistics solutions across Canada and the USA

Comprehensive Freight Solutions

At Craft Logistics, we are proud to be your trusted partner for transportation and freight shipping services across Canada and the United States. Our team of dedicated freight logistics consultants and brokers is well-connected with a vast network of carriers nationwide. With our expertise in handling documents, insurance, and selecting the most suitable shipping method for your cargo, you can trust us to deliver exceptional service.

Full Truckload (FTL)

Cost‑effective and dependable FTL freight shipping services across Canada and the USA. Our full truckload solutions are ideal for large shipments requiring dedicated capacity, ensuring faster transit times and reduced handling. With a network of trusted carriers, we provide secure, on‑time deliveries tailored to your business needs, backed by real‑time tracking and 24/7 support.

Volume LTL

Affordable less‑than‑truckload solutions with the same reliability as full truckload freight. Perfect for smaller shipments, our LTL services maximize efficiency by consolidating cargo, reducing costs while maintaining speed and safety. We coordinate with trusted carriers to ensure flexible scheduling, transparent pricing, and dependable delivery across North America.

Produce Transportation

Specialized handling of perishable goods with timely delivery and expert coordination. Our produce transport services use temperature‑controlled equipment and strict quality standards to preserve freshness. With proactive monitoring and experienced drivers, we guarantee your produce reaches its destination in optimal condition.

Heavy Haul & Flatbed

Secure and efficient delivery of oversize freight with proven heavy haul drivers. Our flatbed solutions accommodate large, irregular, or heavy cargo with specialized equipment and safety protocols. We manage permits, routing, and compliance to ensure smooth transport of even the most challenging loads.

Power-Only Freight

Reliable coordination of carriers for safe and timely power‑only shipments nationwide. Whether you need tractors to move your trailers or flexible capacity for seasonal demand, our power‑only solutions provide dependable drivers and equipment. We streamline scheduling and tracking to keep your operations running smoothly.

Refrigerated Freight

Temperature‑sensitive freight solutions with trusted refrigerated carriers across the country. Our reefer services ensure consistent climate control for frozen, chilled, or perishable goods. With real‑time monitoring and strict compliance standards, we safeguard product integrity from pickup to delivery.
